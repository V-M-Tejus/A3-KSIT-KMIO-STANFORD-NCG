RHS 25

Determinants of Delay from Registration to treatment initiation in Lung and Colorectal Cancer Patients in Kidwai Memorial Institute of Oncology, Bengaluru.

KMIO is a tertiary cancer care center catering to a large number of patients Massive work load, limited man power, saturated services combined with patients from poor educational back ground makes delay at every level. Lung and Colorectal Cancer is one among the ten leading sites of Cancer incidence among both men and women in Cancer registry, Bengaluru. The delay in diagnosis and treatment initiation represents a multifaceted problem attributable to multiple factors. Quantifying the treatment delay helps us to understand the potential factors responsible and reducing it for better Patient care.

Duration: From April-2022 to December-2022

RHS 25

Identify Target / Actual / Gap:

We analyzed the data of Lung and Colorectal Cancer. Patients registered at KMIO from April 2022 and was found that of 30 patients, currently the median treatment initiation time for Lung and Colorectal Cancer Patients is 26 and 28 days respectively, with minimum of 7 days and maximum of 68 days.

SMART Goal

Reducing treatment initiation time for Lung and Colorectal cancer patients by 50% of current time within December 2022.

RHS 25

Interventions

Specialist consultation at KMIO:

The patients had to visit other super specialty hospitals like Jayadeva Institute of Cardiology, and Institute of Nephro-Urology for cardiac and renal evaluation, expert opinion, interventional procedures if required. KMIO has appointed Cardiologist, Nephrologist/ Urologist for full time to cater to the Cardiac and Renal issues. Previously patients were visiting above centres to take expert opinion, these centers are 3 to 6 km away from the Institute. 3-5 days were Spent to seek the appointments and obtain expert opinion.

RHS 25

Outcome of Project

  • The waiting Period for treatment initiation of lung cancer has reduced from 26 days to 16 days. For Colorectal cancer the treatment initiation time has reduced from 28 days to 17 days.
  • The economic and Social Burden on patient and attenders has reduced due to less number of hospital visits and decreased duration of stay for out station patients.
  • Maximum utilization of hospital resources

Additional Results

  • Waiting time for treatment initiation for all other subsites of cancer has reduced because of the interventions.
  • Out comes from the decrease in the treatment initiation time of all Cancers must be evaluated.
  • Treatment drop outs for the outstation patients has reduced.

Key Learning points from Project

  • If we know the exact cause, it is easy to find the solution.
  • The scientific way of identification of root cause helps to convince the Administration and bring about better changes in the hospital.
  • Small modifications in the routine procedure will yield good results and there by improving the patient care.
  • Implementation of Phase-II intervention completely.
  • Sustainability of the modified changes with continuous efforts.
  • Cost effectiveness (Direct & Indirect) from all the above interventions needs to be evaluated.
  • New Projects to be taken up and solve other unforeseen problems.
  • Constitution and Institutionalization of Quality Circles
